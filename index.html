Frequently Asked Questions

Como eu sei qual English Course é o melhor para mim?

Nós recomendamos começar com um nivelamento gratuito com um de nossos coordenadores. Eles avaliarão seu nível atual de English fluency, entender seus objetivos e recomendar o melhor curso para você. Seja por um BOOST na carreira, viagem, ou propósitos acadêmicos, nós temos programas especialmente criados para cada objetivo.

Todos os professores são falantes de English nativos?

Yes, all of our teachers are certified native English speakers from countries like the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. They have TEFL/TESOL certification and a minimum of 3 years of teaching experience. Many also specialize in Business English, TOEFL preparation, or conversation skills.

Quais os valores dos cursos?

Our course prices vary depending on the program, intensity, and duration. We offer flexible pricing options starting from $15 per hour for group classes and $25 per hour for one-on-one sessions. We also provide package discounts for students who commit to longer courses. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.

Como são as English classes online?

Our online classes are conducted via our secure video conferencing platform. You'll need a computer or tablet with a camera, microphone, and stable internet connection. All learning materials are provided through our digital learning platform, which you can access 24/7. Classes combine live instruction, interactive exercises, and practical conversation practice.

Oferecem uma garantia de satisfação?

Absolutely! We're confident in our teaching methods and guarantee your satisfaction. If you complete your assignments and attend all scheduled classes but don't see improvement in your English skills within the first month, we'll provide additional classes at no extra cost. If you're still not satisfied, we offer a prorated refund for unused classes.
